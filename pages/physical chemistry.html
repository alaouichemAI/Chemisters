Evolution of Physical Chemistry

Key milestones that shaped our understanding of molecular behavior and energy transformations.

1850s
Birth of Thermodynamics
Clausius and Kelvin formulate the laws of thermodynamics, establishing the foundations for understanding energy transformations in chemical systems.
1889
Arrhenius Equation
Svante Arrhenius develops his temperature-dependent rate equation, revolutionizing chemical kinetics and reaction rate understanding.
1926
Quantum Mechanics Revolution
Schr√∂dinger publishes his wave equation, providing the mathematical framework for understanding molecular structure and bonding at the quantum level.
1965
Density Functional Theory
Hohenberg and Kohn establish DFT foundations, later earning Kohn the Nobel Prize and transforming computational chemistry capabilities.
1999
Femtochemistry Era
Ahmed Zewail wins Nobel Prize for femtosecond spectroscopy, enabling direct observation of chemical bonds forming and breaking in real time.
2020s
AI-Enhanced Physical Chemistry
Machine learning algorithms predict molecular properties, reaction pathways, and thermodynamic parameters with unprecedented accuracy and speed.
